Srambled Eggs

Description

This is a recipe to cook scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat a pan and coat bottom with a thin layer of vegitable oil.
  2. Crack 4 eggs into pan quickly.
  3. Move eggs around with spatula while they cook.
  4. Once solid, add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Move to a plate and serve.
