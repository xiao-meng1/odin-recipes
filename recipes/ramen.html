Ramen

Description

This is a recipe to cook storebought instant ramen

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of ramen
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Add seasoning from ramen pack.
  3. Crack 2 eggs into the pot and boil for 2 minutes.
  4. Add ramen noodles into the pot and boil for 3 minutes.
  5. Pour into bowl and serve.
